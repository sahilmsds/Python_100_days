Best Movies According to Sahil

My top 3 movies of all time.

96

A tamil language romantic movie starring Vijay Sethupati and Trisha Krishnan.Two high school sweethearts meet at a reunion after 22 years and reminisce about their past.

I feel this kind of movies can lift my mood anytime.


Kantara

A Kannada language action thriller movie starring Rishab Shetty. When greed paves the way for betrayal, scheming and murder, a young tribal reluctantly dons the traditions of his ancestors to seek justice.

This is absolute cinema. Hats off to Rishab Shetty.


Mast challay amcha

A marathi language comedy movie starring my all time favorite The Bharat Jadhav, Bhushan Kadu and Vishaka Subhedar.Love and hate relationship between a bank manager Father in law and an auto rickshaw driver Son in law where both pretend to be one above the other.

I must have watched this movie more than 3k times. I personally recommend this movie those who understand marathi for sure.
